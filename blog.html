Modern Residential Architecture – SketchUp Modeling Study

These figures present a series of modern residential design explorations modeled in SketchUp, focusing on clean geometry, material contrast, and the relationship between architecture and its natural surroundings. The intention of this project was to develop realistic architectural forms while studying light, proportion, and spatial experience.

Overall Design Concept

The architectural language is minimalist and contemporary, defined by simple volumes, flat roofs, and strong horizontal lines. The massing is composed of clear rectangular forms that create a calm and balanced composition. Large openings and cantilevered elements give the buildings a lightweight appearance while enhancing visual connection with the landscape.

Material and Texture Strategy

A restrained material palette was used to emphasize elegance and clarity:

  • Timber cladding adds warmth and contrast to the modern form.
  • Glass façades create transparency, allowing natural light to penetrate deep into the interior spaces.
  • Concrete and neutral-toned surfaces ground the structure and reinforce a modern aesthetic.

The contrast between solid and transparent elements helps define private and public zones while maintaining visual continuity.

Relationship with the Site

The houses are carefully integrated into a natural, green environment. Elevated platforms and stepped access paths respond to the terrain and reduce visual impact on the landscape. Surrounding vegetation is used not only as a backdrop but as an active design element that softens the architecture and enhances the sense of privacy.

Lighting and Atmosphere

Day and evening scenes are used to explore how light transforms the architecture:

  • Daytime views highlight form, materiality, and spatial clarity.
  • Evening and night scenes emphasize artificial lighting, showcasing how warm interior lights contrast with the darker surroundings and create a welcoming atmosphere.

Exterior lighting is subtle and strategically placed to enhance depth, edges, and circulation paths without overpowering the design.

Modeling Focus

This project demonstrates:

  • Precise SketchUp modeling techniques
  • Clean geometry and accurate proportions
  • Attention to architectural detail
  • Consideration of realism through lighting, reflections, and environmental context

Architectural Visualizations

3D renderings and architectural visualizations created using Rhinoceros 3D with V-Ray rendering, demonstrating materiality, lighting dynamics, and spatial relationships

Interior Visualization
Visualization 01

Sunlight-Optimized Living Space

This comprehensive study explores the dynamic relationship between architecture and natural light throughout different times of the day. The project demonstrates how strategic design decisions can optimize daylight penetration while creating dramatic visual effects.

The space features polished concrete floors, oak wood wall paneling, and a minimalist color palette of warm greys and beige. Strategically placed clerestory windows create dramatic light patterns while maintaining privacy and controlling glare.

Using Rhinoceros 3D with V-Ray rendering, this visualization accurately simulates real-world lighting conditions and material responses to changing light throughout the day.

Exterior Visualization
Visualization 02

Contemporary Villa Design

Modern residential design exploring material honesty, clean architectural lines, and the relationship between solid and void. Features textured concrete, expansive glass surfaces, and thermally modified wood cladding.

The cantilevered roof element serves both functional and aesthetic purposes, providing solar shading while creating dramatic shadows that change throughout the day, adding dynamic visual interest to the façade.

This visualization study examines the integration of architecture with its natural surroundings, focusing on material texture, shadow play, and environmental responsiveness.

Minimalist Residential Form
